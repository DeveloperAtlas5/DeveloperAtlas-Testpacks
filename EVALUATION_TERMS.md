# Evaluation Terms

These public packs are shared for early evaluation and personal testing.

## You may

- download and test the public packs
- share feedback
- use the packs for personal learning/testing
- link to this repository

## Please do not

- resell the packs
- repackage them as your own product
- imply official certification, partnership, or endorsement
- treat preview packs as complete courses
- upload private Full Pack or Dev Pack material publicly

## Private material

The full Developer Atlas system, generator tools, complete Canon library, internal reports, and Dev Pack remain private unless explicitly released later.

## Feedback permission

By sending feedback, you allow the project maintainer to summarize it for product improvement.

Private names, emails, or sensitive details should not be published without permission.
