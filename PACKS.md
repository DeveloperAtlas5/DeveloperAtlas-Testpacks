# Developer Atlas Pack Ladder

Developer Atlas uses small packs so feedback stays focused.

## Public paths

### 1. Beginner Preview

For people who are new to web development or unsure how to start a project.

Current status: preview  
Main question: are the first-start instructions clear enough?

### 2. Rookie Pack

For people who already have a basic Vue project or are ready to build a simple to-do app.

Current status: fully testable public pack  
Main question: can the tester complete one calm Vue mission?

### 3. Library Preview

For people who want quick explanations of programming concepts.

Current status: preview  
Main question: do concept cards feel useful and easy to scan?

### 4. AI Collaboration Preview

For people who use AI coding tools and want better control.

Current status: preview  
Main question: do guardrails help AI assist without taking over?

## Private paths for now

These remain private/internal:

- Full Pack
- Dev Pack
- complete Concept Library source
- complete Canon system
- generator tools
- validators
- internal reports

## Why this ladder exists

Different people get stuck in different places.

A beginner may need help opening a project.

A rookie may need one concrete mission.

A builder may need explanations.

An AI-assisted coder may need scope control.

The pack ladder helps people choose the right surface instead of seeing the entire system at once.
