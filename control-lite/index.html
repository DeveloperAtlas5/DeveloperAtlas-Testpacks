LOCAL-FIRST PUBLIC PREVIEW

Atlas Control Lite

Define what may change, inspect what actually changed, record observed evidence, and keep the final decision human.

Runs in this browser No account, telemetry, AI connection, or command execution.

Step 1

Prepare a bounded change

State the outcome before discussing implementation. One line per boundary or criterion.

Step 2

Review the AI instruction

This instruction adds scope, protected behavior, acceptance criteria, and verification honesty to the human goal.

Human checkpoint: edit the preparation fields when the instruction permits too much, protects too little, or asks for unverifiable proof.

Step 3

Inspect scope and impact

Paste the exact changed-file list and an optional patch or summary. Control Lite compares paths; it does not claim to understand or execute the patch.

Step 4

Record observed evidence

Passed means the result was actually observed. Leave an unavailable or unobserved check as Not run.

Step 5

Accept, revise, or reject

Atlas shows whether the selected decision aligns with the recorded scope and evidence. It does not choose for you.

Final decision

Step 6

Preserve the controlled-change record

Review the generated Markdown before saving it beside the change or attaching it to a pull request.
