# Vision

Developer Atlas exists to help people remain responsible for software created
with AI assistance.

The intended experience is not “ask AI and hope.” It is:

1. state the outcome and boundaries;
2. convert human and project preferences into a scoped instruction;
3. keep the resulting change traceable;
4. understand what changed and what may be affected;
5. review objective verification evidence;
6. accept, revise, or reject the result; and
7. preserve the accepted decision for future work.

The long-term opportunity is a model-independent control and comprehension
layer that works with the developer's chosen AI tool while keeping project
context local by default.
