# Provenance and AI assistance

Developer Atlas is a human-directed, materially AI-assisted engineering and
research project.

AI assistance has been used for activities including structured drafting,
implementation support, claim extraction, primary-source mapping, test design,
and review preparation. That assistance does not replace human ownership or
independent review.

## Evidence boundary

- Automated tests establish repeatable technical checks.
- AI-assisted primary-source review establishes traceability, not independent
  certification.
- Independent human node review remains incomplete.
- The human maintainer owns scope decisions and final acceptance.
- Public claims deliberately distinguish working software from market evidence.

## Public-export boundary

This repository is a reviewed export. It excludes private strategy, raw AI
conversations, personal data, credentials, private project source, internal
feedback, databases, model files, and the complete private product source.
